About ElectroVision 2K25

ElectroVision 2K25 is a premier technical event hosted by the Department of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering at Rajashri Shahu College of Engineering. Our mission is to provide a platform for young engineers to innovate, compete, and showcase their talents in circuit design, troubleshooting, technical quizzes, and hands-on projects.

This event is designed to bridge the gap between theoretical learning and practical implementation, fostering a spirit of creativity and problem-solving among students.
